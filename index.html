Saipan's Must try Restaurants

Food is something you always must experience when travel, so in this artcle, I would like to give you my top picks on where to dine when you are visiting Saipan.

Best places to dine:

  1. Zoom Café
  2. Shrley's coffe shop
  3. Mango Six
  4. Etc
  5. Etc

Zoom Café. | Susupe, Saipan

Zoom Café serves great fried chicken which is my personal favorite. They also serve a number of drinks and other types of desserts. They also have karaoke rooms that you and your friends could enjoy.

What i love:

  • Their chicken wings
  • Not Too Expensive
  • Their Karaoke room
